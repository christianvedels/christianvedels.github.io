# Research

## Working Papers

### A Perfect Storm: First-Nature Geography and Economic Development

Is geography destiny? This project estimates the effect of a major natural shock to navigable access in northwestern Denmark and shows large impacts on market access and population.

- Working paper: https://arxiv.org/abs/2408.00885
- Replication files: https://github.com/christianvedels/A_perfect_storm_replication
- Talk video: https://youtu.be/f7Xo7BO_Xqk?si=xMAS9viHL34BGbWt

### Breaking the HISCO Barrier: Automatic Occupational Standardization with OccCANINE

With Christian Moller-Dahl and Torben Johansen.

This project introduces OccCANINE, a model for automatic HISCO classification of occupational descriptions across multiple languages.

- Working paper: https://arxiv.org/abs/2402.13604
- Project repository: https://github.com/christianvedels/OccCANINE
