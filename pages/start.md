# Christian Vedel

I am an Assistant Professor at the Historical Economics and Development Group (HEDG) at the Department of Economics, University of Southern Denmark.

I study how geography and institutions shape living conditions, using causal inference and machine learning with historical georeferenced data.

Email: Christian-vs@sam.sdu.dk

## Highlights

- New Researcher Prize (Economic History Society, 2023)
- Current focus: geography, institutions, and long-run development
- Working at the intersection of economic history and data science
